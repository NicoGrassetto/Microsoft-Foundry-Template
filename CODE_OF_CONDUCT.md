# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful, constructive, and patient.
- Welcome differing viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and receive feedback with care.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and project outcomes rather than people.
- Respect privacy, security, and confidentiality.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, sustained disruption, or insulting comments.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, tenant details, or other sensitive information.
- Any conduct that maintainers reasonably determine to be inappropriate for a collaborative project space.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying expectations and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that do not follow this Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or its community.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately when possible. If a private channel is not available, open a public issue with only enough information to request maintainer contact. Do not include private personal information, secrets, or security-sensitive details in public reports.
